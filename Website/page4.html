Why Delta?

  

People

Delta people are those who you want to have brunch with Sunday morning. They are the ones you can work out or play sports with after class. The ones you want to talk to anytime you have questions or concerns.

  

Culture

No toxicity involved. We are passionate individuals who all care about the mission of the club. Disregarding majors and backgrounds, we want those who can contribute to this special type of environment we have fostered.

  

Focus

We are a consulting club with an emphasis on education. With a specific focus unlike any other consulting club, our projects bring high value to both our members and the companies we serve.
